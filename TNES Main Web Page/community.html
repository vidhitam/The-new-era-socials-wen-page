What we do together

TNES organizes recurring activities that make it easy to plug in: seasonal service days, neighborhood meetups, skill-sharing workshops, and support for local schools and nonprofits. Each initiative is led or co-led by volunteers, with staff helping coordinate logistics, safety, and partner relationships. Whether you can give an hour or a season, there is a place for you on the calendar.
