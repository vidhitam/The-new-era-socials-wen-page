Our club & New Era Socials

New Era Socials is our volunteer community: a club where neighbors meet, plan activities, and support local causes with energy and care.

What is New Era Socials?

It is the home for people who want to give time without bureaucracy—weekend projects, mentorship, events, and partnerships that keep our area connected. Whether you can offer an hour or a season, you belong here.

Mission

We mobilize volunteers and partners to meet community needs with dignity and efficiency—coordinating people, resources, and ideas so service stays accessible, organized, and welcoming.

Vision

Neighborhoods where helping is a habit—residents, schools, and organizations working together, with a clear path for every new volunteer to grow their skills over time.

Values

What we practice in how we plan, communicate, and show up.

Compassion

Empathy and respect for everyone we serve and every volunteer we support.

Integrity

Transparent with partners, accountable for outcomes, careful with trust.

Inclusion

Opportunities that welcome diverse backgrounds, abilities, and schedules.

Excellence

Thoughtful preparation, safe operations, and meaningful experiences.
